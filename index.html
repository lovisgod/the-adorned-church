We are the Ardoned

Living in God's Amazing Grace!

Come and Fellowship with us!

Everyone understands family, either by cheer experience or inexperience. It is irreplaceable. We are one big family, a people who believe its okay for you not to be okay, Who offer you the warmth of healing and restoration, who kindle and preserve the fire of revival, Who build ourselves unto our inheritance actualised by walking in Dominion. So, none of us is under pressure, the presence of each member creates an atmosphere for everyone to grow and walk in total freedom which was bought us by our Lord.

Services

Counseling

We offer counselling service to intending couples, entrepreneur and students, making use of biblical principles.

Training

We help people to achieve their dreams and visions through training that are focused on lifestyle and Leadership.

Outreaches

We reach out to the unsaved through preaching of the Gospel. We help and encourage people to pray through programs organized by the church extended Ministries.
